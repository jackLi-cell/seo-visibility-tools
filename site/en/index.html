FAQ

Will these tools upload my data?
No. All tools run entirely in your browser. Your HTML, URL lists, and other data are never sent to any server.
How long until a page marked "Discovered - not indexed" gets indexed?
There is no fixed timeline. Google decides based on page quality, site authority, and crawl budget. Fixing technical issues can improve indexing probability.
What is the difference between AI search engines and traditional search engines?
AI search engines rely more on structured content, clear semantic markup, and directly extractable answers. Traditional SEO keyword density strategies have limited effect on AI search.
What does self-referencing canonical mean?
Self-referencing canonical means the page's canonical tag points to its own URL. This tells search engines "this is the official version of this content" and is a recommended practice.
