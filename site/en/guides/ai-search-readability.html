How to Improve AI Search Readability

Optimization methods to make pages effectively understood by AI search engines and summarization tools

How AI Search Engines Understand Pages

AI 搜索引擎（如 ChatGPT 搜索、Perplexity、Google AI Overview）与传统搜索引擎不同。它们需要从页面中提取可直接引用的答案，而不只是匹配关键词。

What AI Search Engines Value

  • Clear content structure (heading hierarchy, distinct paragraphs)
  • Directly extractable answers (definitions, steps, lists)
  • Authority signals (author, source, update date)
  • Structured data (JSON-LD, especially FAQ)
  • Content timeliness (dateModified)
  • Sufficient content depth

Optimization Methods

  1. Each page should have exactly one H1 that clearly expresses the topic
  2. Use H2/H3 to organize content hierarchy
  3. Provide a concise summary or definition at the beginning
  4. Use ordered lists for steps, unordered lists for key points
  5. Add FAQ structured data
  6. Mark author information and last update date
  7. Ensure body text exceeds 300 words
  8. Add internal links to related content
